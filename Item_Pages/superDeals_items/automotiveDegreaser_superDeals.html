Description

Simple Green MAX was developed with the auto enthusiast in mind. This heavy duty pro-strength automotive cleaner and degreaser was specifically formulated to handle your toughest cleaning jobs. Powers away oil, brake dust, grease, and grime. The non-abrasive formula is ideal for all types of vehicles, tools, equipment, garages and shops. Removes dirt, grease, tar, asphalt, mud, black streaks, transmission fluid, metal-working fluids, and more. Cleans a variety of surfaces without high-pH, harsh or hazardous caustic chemicals that can degrade surfaces and finishes.



Non-abrasive formula cleans without scratching or stripping OEM automotive finishes
For use on all types of vehicles: cars, motorcycles, RVs, trucks, ATVs and trailers
Versatile formula, for use on oxidized paint, aluminum, chrome and other metals, carbon fiber, fiberglass and composites
Effectively cleans acrylic and clear coat
Free of harmful ammonia and isopropyl alcohol which can cause reactions on skin and delicate surfaces
Does not require personal protective gear when in use
